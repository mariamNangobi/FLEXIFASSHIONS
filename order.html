How to Place an Order on Jumia

Follow these simple steps to shop on Jumia Uganda and get your items delivered to your doorstep

1

Find Your Product

Browse through our categories or use the search bar to find the product you want to purchase. You can filter by price, brand, and customer ratings.

2

Add to Cart

Click on "Add to Cart" button when you've found the product you want to purchase. You can continue shopping or proceed to checkout.

3

Proceed to Checkout

Review your cart (quantity, color, size) and click on "Proceed to Checkout" when you're ready to complete your order.

4

Enter Delivery Details

Provide your delivery address and contact information where you want to receive your order. You can save multiple addresses.

5

Select Payment Method

Choose your preferred payment option: Mobile Money, Credit Card, JumiaPay, or Cash on Delivery.

6

Place Your Order

Review your order details and click "Place Order" to complete your purchase. You'll receive an order confirmation email/SMS.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?
You can track your order by logging into your Jumia account and going to "My Orders". You'll receive SMS and email updates with your delivery status. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service for assistance.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Mobile Money (MTN, Airtel), Visa/Mastercard, JumiaPay, and Cash on Delivery for most items. Some items may not be eligible for Cash on Delivery.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary by location: Kampala (1-3 days), Other major towns (3-5 days), Upcountry (5-7 days). Some items may take longer depending on availability.
Can I return or exchange an item?
Yes, we offer a 7-day free return policy for most items. The item must be unused, in its original packaging with all tags attached. Some items like perishables and personal care products may not be returnable.
